🎯 Programs & Activities

Fun-Filled Learning Activities

Every activity is thoughtfully designed to spark curiosity, build skills, and create lasting memories for your little ones.

🎨01

Drawing & Art

Unleash creativity through painting, clay modeling, and mixed media art. Children develop fine motor skills and self-expression through colorful masterpieces.

🖌️ Creative Arts
💃02

Dance & Movement

Classical, folk, and freestyle dance sessions help children develop coordination, rhythm, confidence, and a lifelong love for performing arts.

🎵 Performing Arts
🎹03

Music & Rhymes

Keyboard, rhythm instruments, and vocal training nurture musical intelligence. Singing nursery rhymes builds language skills and emotional development.

🎼 Music Education
📖04

Storytelling & Drama

Interactive story sessions, puppet shows, and mini plays build vocabulary, imagination, empathy, and public speaking confidence in every child.

🎭 Language Arts
🏃05

Sports & Games

Outdoor games, yoga, gymnastics, and team sports develop physical health, sportsmanship, leadership, and collaborative spirit in young minds.

⚽ Physical Education
🔬06

STEM Discovery Lab

Hands-on science experiments, number puzzles, and coding basics introduce children to STEM concepts through playful, inquiry-based exploration sessions.

🧪 STEM Learning

Frequently Asked Questions

We accept children from 2 years onwards for Playgroup, 3 years for Nursery, 4 years for LKG, and 5 years for UKG. Children must meet the age criteria as of June 1st of the admission year.
We maintain a maximum ratio of 15:1 (students to teacher) to ensure personalized attention and a nurturing environment. Each class also has a dedicated assistant teacher.
Yes! We have GPS-tracked, air-conditioned school buses covering major areas around Tiruppur. Each bus has a dedicated female attendant for children's safety. Routes can be discussed during admission.
We provide daily activity reports via our parent app, monthly progress portfolios with photos, quarterly report cards, and parent-teacher meetings every term. You're always in the loop!
Our campus has 24/7 CCTV surveillance, secure entry/exit with ID verification, trained first-aid staff, child-safe furniture, and regular safety drills. Every child's safety is our top priority.
